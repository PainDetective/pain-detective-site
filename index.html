About the Book

*The Pain Detective* is your guide to solving the mystery of chronic pain. Through a unique noir-inspired narrative, this two-volume self-help series empowers you to identify pain-causing habits and reclaim your life. Volume 1 explores the H.E.L.L. theory and musculoskeletal pain, while Volume 2 dives into organ-related issues. With practical tools and engaging case stories, it’s pain relief with a detective’s edge.
